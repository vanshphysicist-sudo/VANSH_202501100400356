About Darjeeling :

Darjeeling, located in the Indian state of West Bengal, is a picturesque hill station known for its tea gardens, stunning views of the Himalayas, and colonial architecture. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Top Attractions in Darjeeling :

  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this narrow-gauge railway offers a scenic ride through the hills.
  • Tiger Hill: Famous for its breathtaking sunrise views over the Kanchenjunga mountain range.
  • Batasia Loop: A unique railway loop with beautiful gardens and panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
  • Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park: Home to rare and endangered species like the snow leopard and red panda.
  • Tea Gardens: Visit the lush tea estates and learn about the tea-making process.
  • Peace Pagoda: A serene Buddhist stupa offering stunning views of Darjeeling and the Himalayas.
