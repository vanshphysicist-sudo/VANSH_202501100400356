About Chennai :

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and stunning beaches. It is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions in Chennai :

  • Marina Beach: One of the longest urban beaches in the world, perfect for a relaxing stroll.
  • Fort St. George: A historic fort built by the British East India Company in 1644.
  • Kapaleeshwarar Temple: A stunning Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Santhome Cathedral Basilica: A beautiful church built over the tomb of St. Thomas, one of the twelve apostles of Jesus.
  • Government Museum: One of the oldest museums in India, showcasing a vast collection of artifacts and art.
  • Chennai Rail Museum: A fascinating museum displaying the history of Indian railways with vintage locomotives and carriages.
